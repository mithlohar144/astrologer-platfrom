Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: 09 Sep 2025

1. Consultation Services

All consultations are personalized and time-based services. Once a session is completed or materials are delivered, fees are non-refundable.

2. Cancellation

You may reschedule your consultation up to 12 hours before the scheduled time without any additional fee. For cancellations within 12 hours of the appointment, the fee may be forfeited.

3. No-Show Policy

If you fail to attend the scheduled session without prior notice, the session will be considered consumed and no refund will be provided.

4. Technical Issues

If a session cannot be conducted due to technical issues on our end, we will reschedule the session at no additional cost.

5. Payment Disputes

For any payment related concerns, please contact us at info@astrodrkanhaiya.com within 48 hours of the transaction.

6. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact us at info@astrodrkanhaiya.com.
